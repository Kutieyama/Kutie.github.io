About

Salyu

Salyu (サリュ, Saryu) is a miko and the granddaughter of a local priest. She becomes a part-time waitress at the Humming Dining as her grandfather asks the Manager to give her a job.

Appearance

Amon has long blonde hair with violet eyes. She wears a white shirt and a black skirt. She also has a black hairpin. When entering her true form, she is growing taller. She gains a pair of horns, and her breasts are bigger. In this form, Amon wears a black lingerie, a pair of long gloves, and black stockings.
