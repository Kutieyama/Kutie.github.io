Floor ni Maou ga Imasu (フロアに魔王がいます, Furoa ni Maō ga Imasu, lit. "There's a Demon Lord on the Floor") is a Japanese manga series written by Hato and drawn by Kawakami Masaki. It has been serialized in Monthly Comic Alive since 2014.
